The Gallery

Located in the heart of Plateau Mont-Royal, the gallery serves as a showroom and workshop. The atelier has been open since 2006, run by its owner and artist, Jonathan Bourassa.

All frames are made from Imported Italian hand made wood, put together in Montreal at Cadres Supérieurs with the highest grade component to ensure longevity of the art work.

Current Exposition: “Dans ma tête” – “In My Head”

Jonathan Bourassa

Born in 1979, I started my creative journey at the age of 15. Multidisciplinary artist – tattoos and painting canvas have been my main passions and obsessions. You will find in my world, large œuvres with very simple, pure subjects.

My paintings reflect how I want to feel when looking at art — peaceful, calm, marvelled and pure are among some of those sentiments.

Now you can too discover what’s in my head in my new gallery space.
